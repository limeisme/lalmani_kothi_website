The HistoryOf Kashi Vishwanath

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, has a rich history marked by multiple destructions and reconstructions. Originally built by King Vikramaditya, it was later rebuilt by various rulers, including a Gujarati merchant and Rani Ahilyabai Holkar, after being destroyed during different periods
